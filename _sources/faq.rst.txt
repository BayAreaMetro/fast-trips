
FAQ
==================

How do I restart a run after pathfinding?
  Use the option ``pathfinding_type=file``, via ` ``runTest.py`` <scripts/runTest.py>`_ or in the configuration.  Then, drop the ``pathsfound_paths.csv`` and ``pathsfound_links.csv`` files in the output directory for your run, and they'll be read in instead of generated.
